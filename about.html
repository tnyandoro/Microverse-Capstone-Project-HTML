About The Renenewable Convention

Solar Power Events supports the industry through empowering business. Presented by the Solar Energy Industries Association (SEIA) and the Smart Electric Power Alliance (SEPA), it’s the premier events series for the North American solar and energy storage markets. Anchored by the flagship events, Solar Power International (SPI) and Energy Storage International (ESI), SPI, ESI, and North America Smart Energy Week is the largest gathering of solar, energy storage, hydrogen, fuel cell, microgrid, electric vehicle, and wind energy professionals in North America. Solar Power Events also provides an extensive portfolio of regional events featuring a robust education program vetted by industry experts across the renewable energy ecosystem and provides ample networking opportunities at every show. In 2020, Solar Power Events is bringing new shows to Cape Town, South Africa, and more. Coronavirus Update: Our main concern is the health and safety of attendees, exhibitors, and staff. While Solar Power Events is eager to bring the industry’s leading business platform to a city near you, we're taking direction from global, federal, and local health officials leading up to each event. We encourage exhibitors and attendees to check the specific event page for regular updates and sign up for email notifications to receive the most recent updates..
