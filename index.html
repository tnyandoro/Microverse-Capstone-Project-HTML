WELCOME TO THE 2020 SADC RENEWABLE ENERGY CONVENTION

The biennial conference is hosted by SADCRE and World Bank’s Lighting Global Program. According to Mr Mutezo SADCRE’s EA representative, the forum will allow manufacturers, investors and distributors to review the latest technology. Moreover, the delegates in the biennial event will discuss how policy can promote access to clean solar energy.
